Cheesy 5-Layer Lasagna

Ingredients needed

  • 2 cups of canned tomatoe sauce
  • 15-ounces of ricotta cheese
  • 2 extra-large eggs
  • 2 cups freshly grated pecorino romano
  • 1/2 cup chopped parsley
  • 1 pound mozzarella, grated
  • 16 sheets of fresh lasagna noodles

Cooking Instructions

  1. Start by boiling the sheets of pasta and tomatoe sauce in two seperate pots
  2. Mix the ricotta, mozzarella, and parsley into a bowl and set aside
  3. Once the sauce and sheets are ready, take them off the heat and start layering everything in an empty pan
  4. Once the layers have been made simply put it in the oven and wait 30 minutes
