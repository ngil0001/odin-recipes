Lemon Chicken Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless chicken breast
  • 1 quart of heavy whipping cream
  • 1 whole lemon
  • half a stick of butter
  • olive oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat up a skillet on high with olive oil
  2. Once skillet is hot, put in the two chicken breast and brown one side
  3. Once one side is browned, flip chicken over and turn down the heat to medium and cover.
  4. Once chicken reaches internal temp of 165 degrees, take the chicken out.
  5. Keeping the left over residue in the pan you want to add your butter.
  6. Once the butter fully melts and in the Heavy whipping cream. Lightly stir until sauce becomes more viscus
  7. Once the sauce is at the thickness you like squeeze in at least half a lemon. Add more to taste.
  8. Plate chicken and pour sauce evenly between the two. Enjoy!
