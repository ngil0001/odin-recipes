Ribeye

Ingredients

  • 1 ribeye(boneless or bone in)
  • 1/2 stick butter
  • 1 clove of butter
  • thyme
  • olive oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat up cast iron skillet on high with your olive oil
  2. Sear all sides of your ribeye
  3. Once you form a nice sear on all sides lower the heat and put in your butter and garlic
  4. Once the butter melts, Add your thyme on top of the ribeye and slightly tilt the cast iron towards you. Carefully start basting the butter over the steak for a few minutes.
  5. After youre done basting, place your steak in the oven and cook to your prefered temp
